WHAT IS CODERIT?

Education

Learn something new with the help of others. Better your own abilities.

Community

Join like-minded, creative individuals. Be a part of the hackathon movement.

Inclusivity

Help create an open, educative environment. All have the opportunity to learn.

CodeRIT is both a community of students and an opportunity to learn, think, and create. We welcome all who have an interest to expand and apply their education. We strive to be self-starters, inventors, and entrepreneurs. We give you the tools and skills to be better and build your dreams into realities.
